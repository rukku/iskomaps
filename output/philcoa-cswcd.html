to Tandang Sora Hall (CSWCD)

The jeep enters UP Diliman through the University Avenue.



The first landmark you'll see is the checkpoint. This is quite noticeable as all vehicles slowdown at the guard house near the checkpoint.

The jeep then heads towards the Oblation Plaza until it reaches the Academic Oval , a one-way busy thoroughfare lined by lots of huge Acacia trees.

Step off from the jeep at the line of waiting sheds in front of Vinzons Hall. Vinzons is a major transpo hub inside UP as all jeepney routes pass by this area. The UP Sunken Garden is visible from here. You have to transfer to the IKOT jeep at Vinzons.

Along F. M. Guerrero St., the jeep will pass by Romulo Hall (Asian Center).

It will also pass by Ilang-Ilang Residence Hall before turning left at Laurel St.

While at Laurel St., the jeep passes by the UP Chapel.

You will also see the UP Shopping Center (SC) along Laurel St.

The jeep will then turn left at Roces St. where you'll see Kalayaan Residence Hall.

The jeep then turns right at Magsaysay St. You’ll see Molave Residence Hall at Roces cor Magsaysay St.

Tandang Sora Hall (CSWCD)

After the jeep passes the intersection of Magsaysay and Ylanan Sts., you’ll see the Center for Women’s Studies on the other side of the road. Right next to it is the Tandang Sora Hall (CSWCD).
